Summary for GCC-11.2.0

The compiler is now installed under /opt/gcc-11.2.0.

Setting PATH

To launch it, you must put /opt/gcc-11.2.0/bin in front of your PATH environment variable. The following command enables you to do this in both bash and zsh: 

export PATH=/opt/gcc-11.2.0/bin:$PATH

 

Installing GDB
