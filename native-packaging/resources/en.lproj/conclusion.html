Summary for GCC-12.1.0-aarch64

The compiler is now installed under /opt/gcc-12.1.0-aarch64, though you can move it to a location of your choice.

Setting PATH

To launch it, you must put /opt/gcc-12.1.0-aarch64/bin (or, if you've moved it, /where/ever/bin) in front of your PATH environment variable. The following command enables you to do this in both bash and zsh: 

export PATH=/opt/gcc-12.1.0-aarch64/bin:$PATH
